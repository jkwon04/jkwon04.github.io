Dracaena cinnabari

Dragon blood tree

⌂

Height: 30 to 40 ft

Origin: Native to the Socotra Archipelago,
located in the Arabian Sea off the coast of
Yemen

Requirements: Bright, indirect sunlight,
well-draining soil, temperature between
65°F and 75°F, is drought tolerant and
prefers humidity

Lifespan: Up to 600 years
