Gloriosa

Flame lily

⌂

Height: 2 to 3 in

Origin: Tropical and southern Africa,
tropical and temperate Asia

Requirements: Full sun to partial shade,
well-drained soil enriched with compost,
and requires a support structure to
climb on, prefers warm weather, should
be planted after the last frost, needs
consistent moisture during the growing
season, avoid soggy soil, ideally plant the
tubers horizontally in the ground at a
depth of 2 to 4 in apart

Lifespan: Up to decades
