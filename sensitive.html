Mimosa pudica

Sensitive plant

⌂

Height: 1 to 2 in

Origin: Tropical Central and South America,
from Southern Mexico to mid-South
America and the Caribbean

Requirements: Bright, indirect light,
well-draining soil with a slightly acidic to
neutral pH, consistent moisture, avoiding
soggy soil, high humidity, warm
temperatures between 65 to 75 degrees
fahrenheit

Lifespan: 1 to 2 years
