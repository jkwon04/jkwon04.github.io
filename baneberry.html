Actaea pachypoda

White baneberry

⌂

Height: 30 inches

Origin: Eastern North America

Requirements: Moist, well-drained, humus-rich
soil with partial to fill shade, ideally in a
woodland setting; it prefers acidic soil and
should not be allowed to dry out completely,
but also needs good drainage to prevent overly
wet conditions

Lifespan: About a decade, under ideal conditions
