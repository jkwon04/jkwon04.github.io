Geum triflorum

Prairie smoke

⌂

Height: 6 to 18 in

Origin: Southern Canada, Central and
Northern U.S. in temperate and
sub-arctic grasslands

Requirements: Thrives in full sun, prefers
well-drained soil (tolerating a range of soil
types including sandy and clay), and needs
moist conditions in spring while tolerating
drier summers

Lifespan: Up to 5 years
