Thismia rodwayi

Fairy lantern

⌂

Height: 5 to 30 mm

Origin: Australian states of Tasmania,
Victoria and New South Wales, New
Zealand

Requirements: Damp, shady, and wet
eucalypt forest habitat with a thick
layer of leaf litter, deep and
well-developed soil with a substantial
leaf litter cover is crucial for its survival –
saprophyte; derives nutrients from
decaying organic matter in the soil

Lifespan: Early to late spring
